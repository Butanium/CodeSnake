This is a league based challenge.

For this challenge, multiple versions of the same game are available. Once you have proven your skills on this first version, you will access a higher league and extra rules will be unlocked.

  The Goal

CodeSnake is a multiplayer game based on the classic single player snake game. The objective is to reach a size of 12, or to be the last one alive.

  Rules

The game is played on a looping grid (going out of the grid will teleport you on the other side). The Grid is represented by : - '.' for empty cells. - '0' for your snake. - '1' for opponent snake. - 'A' for the apple. - 'W' for a wall. - 'B' for a bonus. // Upper league Each turn, you must output a direction (UP, LEFT, RIGHT, DOWN), moving your snake in this direction. If your head touch a wall or a snake (body or head), you are eliminated. In upper leagues, you can also use bonuses (BOOST, SWAP, CLIMB). Eating an apple will increase your size by 1 (your head move on the apple but your tail will be at the same position on this turn). The game last for 200 turns, or until one of the following condition is true : - 1 player reaches a size of 12 - Only 1 player remains

  Game Input

Input for one game turn
- 1 integer mapSize, the size of the map.
- Next mapSize lines, 1 string row of mapSize char.
- 1 string myDirection, the orientation your snake facing.
- 1 integer mySize, the size of your snake.
- Next mySize lines, 2 integers x and y representing your snake position.
(Note : Your snake body is in output from head to tail).
- 1 string opponentDirection, the orientation your opponent's snake facing.
- 1 integer opponentSize, the size of your opponent's snake.
- Next opponentSize lines, 2 integers x and y representing your opponent's snake position.
- the number of valid actions for this turn, validActionCount.
- Next validActionCount lines: 1 string validMove, containing a valid action you can submit.
Output for one game turn
Line 1: 2 space separated string direction and bonus. (Note : you can output just the direction if you don't wish to use a bonus this turn.)
