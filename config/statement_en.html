The Goal

CodeSnake is a multiplayer game based on the classic single player snake game. The objective is to reach a size of 12, or to be the last one alive.

  Rules

The game is played on a looping grid (going out of the grid will teleport you on the other side). Each turn, you must output a direction (UP, LEFT, RIGHT, DOWN), moving your snake in this direction. If your head touch a wall or a snake (body or head), you are eliminated. In upper leagues, you can also use bonuses (BOOST, SWAP, CLIMB). Eating an apple will increase your size by 1 (your head move on the apple but your tail will be at the same position on this turn). The game last for 200 turns, or until one of the following condition is true : - 1 player reaches a size of 12 - Only 1 player remains
